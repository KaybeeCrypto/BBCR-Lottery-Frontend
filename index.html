COMMIT A verifiable on-chain draw. No rerolls. No insiders. Every outcome is committed to the blockchain before it’s revealed. The result is decided by block data — not by us. If it’s on-chain, it’s already committed.
No wheel. No trust.

Eligible wallets are snapshotted, hashed, and revealed using a future Solana block. Anyone can verify the result.
